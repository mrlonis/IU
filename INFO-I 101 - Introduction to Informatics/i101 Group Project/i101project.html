INFO - I 101: Final Group Project

Problem:

Due to students various financial backgrounds, not everyone has access to technology and as a result availability of computers in the labs is becoming limited. The school cannot accomodate additional labs but needs to provide a solution to students who do not have the means to provide their own computers.

To address this problem, our group considered alternatives to computer labs while still giving students access to computers. It was a trial and error process-- as we discussed possible solutions, we discovered why they would not work. While focusing on the root of the problem, financial issues, we were able to design a viable solution to this problem.
