INFO - I 101: Final Group Project - Solution

The Problem

Many students on campus cannot afford their own computers, but need access to technology for their courses. The school cannot accommodate additional labs, nor can it afford to purchase laptops for its students.

Our solution allows the school to provide students with the technology they need without additional funding.

This program, which we have name BorrowIU could be used at universities all over the world.

Many universities use programs similar to ours and many students take advantage of it. All of the people we interviewed believed this program would be successful.

BorrowIU

  • Laptop computers can be checked out with student ID.
  • Time limits:
    • 6 hours
    • Extended 48 hour loan period
  • Machines will be equipped with standard lab software:
    • Adobe Creative Cloud
    • Microsoft Office Suite
    • And more...
  • Where to borrow:
    • Wells Library
    • IMU
    • Forest
    • McNutt
